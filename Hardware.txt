For Teensy/CC3000, We'll use Serial3 for connection to the BetaBrite:

Teensy RX3 Pin7 --> Max3232BOB R1OUT Pin2
Teensy TX3 Pin8 --> Max3232BOB T1IN  Pin4

Button -- Pin2

The CC3000 Breakout is connected as follows:

GND  -- GND BUS
3V3  -- N/C
VIN  -- 5V BUS
VBEN -- Pin5
IRQ  -- Pin3
CS   -- Pin10 \
MOSI -- Pin11  \  SPI interface
MISO -- Pin12  /
CLK  -- Pin13 /
